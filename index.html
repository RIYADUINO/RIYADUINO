RIYADUINO ignited from a single spark: electronics in Saudi Arabia should be accessible, exciting, and empowering for all. Born in Riyadh from a team of passionate engineers and educators, we saw the gap between brilliant ideas and the complex, frustrating path to bringing them to life.

Our mission: to bridge that gap. We meticulously design, build, and rigorously test every project kit, so you can bypass the troubleshooting and dive straight into what matters: learning, coding, and creating.
