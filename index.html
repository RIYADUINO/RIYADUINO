BUILD. LEARN. INNOVATE. THE FUTURE, FORGED IN KSA.

Go from idea to invention. We are Saudi Arabia's launchpad for creators, hobbyists, and pioneers, offering pre-built electronics DIY projects that let you build, code, and innovate—faster.

From Riyadh,
For Everyone.

RIYADUINO ignited from a single spark: electronics in Saudi Arabia should be accessible, exciting, and empowering for all. Born in Riyadh from a team of passionate engineers and educators, we saw the gap between brilliant ideas and the complex, frustrating path to bringing them to life.

Our mission: to bridge that gap. We meticulously design, build, and rigorously test every DIY project, so you can bypass the troubleshooting and dive straight into what matters: learning, coding, and creating.

Our Core Values

⚡️

Local Empowerment

We are a proud Saudi-based initiative. By providing fast, local delivery and dedicated support, we empower our community to build the future, right here at home.

✔️

Uncompromising Quality

Perfection is our starting line. Every project is expertly assembled and rigorously tested by our engineers. Your success is our metric.

🎓

Education First

Our goal is to spark curiosity and accelerate learning. Our DIY projects are designed as powerful educational tools for students, hobbyists, and professionals alike.
